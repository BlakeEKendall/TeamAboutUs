Michael Milroy

Agile Coach and Software Developer

Michael is a Marine Corps Veteran with strong expertise in signals intelligence collection, analysis, and reporting. Michael enjoys spending time with his wife and children, golfing with his brothers, and watching all things television regarding life in the 1800's. He also spends his daily commute time listening to audiobooks from various industries to enhance his foundational knowledge of business.
